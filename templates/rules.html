Server Rules

General Rules

  1. Stay Updated: Regularly check our Discord for any updates or modifications to the rules.
  2. No Erotic Roleplay: Explicit or sexually suggestive content is strictly prohibited.
  3. No Encouraging Rule Breaking: Do not influence or encourage others to break the rules.
  4. Sensitive Topics: Real-life events such as suicide, terrorism, pandemics, and mental illnesses are not permitted in roleplay.
  5. Roleplay Consent: Roleplay should proceed unless all participants agree to pause or stop.
  6. Appropriate Names: Avoid using explicit or offensive names on any platform.
  7. Zero Tolerance Policy: Racism, trolling, discrimination, homophobia, and sexism are not tolerated.
  8. Derogatory Terms: The use of derogatory language is strictly forbidden.
  9. Respect Across Platforms: Do not engage in harmful behaviour on other platforms.
  10. Content Creator Responsibility: Content creators must actively moderate their communities.

Account and Character Guidelines

  1. Account Responsibility: You are responsible for all activities under your account.
  2. No Buying/Selling Accounts: Trading accounts or valuables is strictly prohibited.
  3. Lost Account Access: If you lose access to your Discord account, you must reapply for whitelist status.
  4. Character Integrity: Maintain realistic character backstories and personalities.
  5. No Unrealistic Characters: Avoid unrealistic character names or appearances.
  6. Character Separation: Keep characters distinct with no shared knowledge.
  7. Camera Usage: Only characters with cameras can use recordings as legal evidence.
  8. Unique Concepts: Special character concepts require staff approval.
  9. Property Management: Inactive properties may be removed after 90 days.
  10. Character Permanent Death: Three or more "no pulse" instances result in permanent character death.

Engagement Standards

  1. No Cop Baiting: Intentionally provoking police without proper roleplay is prohibited.
  2. No Money Simulator Gameplay: Focus on roleplay, not just accumulating wealth.
  3. Cooperation with EMS: Refusing to cooperate with EMS staff can lead to removal.
  4. Quality Roleplay: Maintain high-quality roleplay standards at all times.
  5. No Toxic Behaviour: Including shooting downed bodies, disruptive music, or excessive screaming.
  6. No Hospital Camping: Waiting near hospitals to provoke players is not allowed.
  7. Scene Utilization: Use scenes to enhance roleplay and immersion.
  8. Police Interactions: Provide valid reasoning before engaging in combat with police.
  9. Vehicle Destruction: No random vehicle destruction without proper initiation.

Crime and Gangs

  1. 5-Man Rule: Criminal activities are limited to five participants at a time.
  2. Mechanic Restrictions: No F1 menu usage during criminal activities.
  3. Independent Groups: Groups must operate independently during criminal activities.
  4. Prison Transports: Attacking prison transports is a separate scenario.
  5. No Fake Hostages: All criminal activities must be realistic.
  6. Robbery Planning: Proper planning required for all major robberies.
  7. Robbing Players: Must be done with high-quality roleplay and justified context.
  8. Cooldown Periods: Respect cooldown times between criminal activities.
  9. No Restricted Area Robberies: Areas with teleport options are off-limits.
  10. Scamming Rules: Must be conducted through in-game roleplay with valid reasons.

Cheating and Exploiting

  1. No Unfair Modifications: Prohibited modifications include speed hacks, no night vision, health regen, etc.
  2. Permissible Modifications: Visual enhancements are allowed if they don't provide unfair advantages.
  3. No Macroing: Using third-party software to automate actions is forbidden.
  4. Shadow Settings: All players must have shadows enabled.
  5. No Bug Exploitation: Exploiting client or server bugs is strictly prohibited.
  6. No Spawn Camping: Camping animal and vehicle spawns is not allowed.
  7. No Emote Cooldown Bypass: Circumventing emote cooldowns is prohibited.
  8. Bug Reporting: Report all bugs through Discord.

Consequences

Violation of these rules may result in:

  • Warning
  • Temporary ban
  • Permanent ban
  • Character reset
  • Item/vehicle removal
  • Other appropriate actions as determined by staff

By playing on Vibe City Roleplay, you agree to follow these rules and accept the consequences of breaking them.
